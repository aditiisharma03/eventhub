About Banasthali Vidyapith


Banasthali Vidyapith is one of India’s premier institutions for women's education, founded in 1935. It follows a five-fold educational philosophy focusing on Physical, Practical, Aesthetic, Moral, and Intellectual growth. The university offers diverse programs in Science, Technology, Arts, and Management, integrating modern education with traditional values.

The university is known for its vibrant events, including cultural fests, academic conferences, and sports tournaments. Students actively participate in national-level competitions, innovation challenges, and leadership programs, making Banasthali a hub for holistic development and lifelong learning.




Want to be a Volunteer? Apply Soon!

As a volunteer, you'll gain valuable experience, develop leadership skills, and expand your network. Enjoy exclusive access to behind-the-scenes event planning, receive a certificate of appreciation, and enhance your resume with hands-on teamwork. Plus, you'll be part of a passionate community making a real impact!

Volunteering is a great opportunity to work closely with event organizers, improve your problem-solving abilities, and learn how to manage tasks efficiently under deadlines. You’ll also get to interact with students and professionals, boosting your communication and collaboration skills.

Whether you're looking to contribute your time, build new friendships, or challenge yourself in an exciting role, volunteering will provide you with experiences that last a lifetime. Don't miss out—apply now and be a part of something special!
